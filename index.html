Vegetaria Pizzeria

Authentic Italian Vegetarian Pizza

315 E Montgomery Ave, Ardmore, PA 19003

Vegetaria Pizzeria has been committed to serving delicious pizza using sustainable ingredients since our founding in 34 AD by Jesus's lesser known insectoid little brother, Beezus. We are family owned, and we strive to make a positive impact on your health, well-being, and the environment by partnering with local vendors who supply the fresh ingredients for our plant-based pizzas.
